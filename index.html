Account overviews

Manage your accounts, view high level statistics, quotas and account properties at a glance. Use the dashboard for high level management or to quickly drill into any features offered by the Azure Batch service.

Pool views

See what the nodes in your pool are doing at any given time with with a real-time heat map. Also included are out-of-the box metrics and quick drill-downs to node properties and file explorers.

Job mangament

Quickly track the progress of jobs, view the size of the queue and job throughput and highlight errors. The jobs view is a great place to view the state of progress of work of your tasks.

Task performance

Use graphing tools to understand task runtime distribution to find outliers, hidden issue or performance bottlenecks.
